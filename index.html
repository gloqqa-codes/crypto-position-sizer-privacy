Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Data Collection

Crypto Position Sizer collects minimal data necessary for app functionality:

  • Favorite Coins: Stored locally on your device using SQLite database
  • App Preferences: Settings and user preferences stored locally
  • Cryptocurrency Prices: Real-time data fetched from Binance WebSocket API
  • Purchase Information: For premium features, processed through Google Play Billing

Third-Party Services

  • Binance API: For real-time cryptocurrency price data
  • Appodeal: For displaying advertisements in the free version
  • Google Play Billing: For processing in-app purchases

Data Storage & Security

All user preferences, favorite coins, and app settings are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers. We do not collect personal identification information.

Advertising

The free version displays ads through Appodeal. The premium version removes all advertisements.

In-App Purchases

Premium features can be purchased to remove ads. All payments are processed through Google Play Store.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
